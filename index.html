MY UX SKILLS

USER RESEARCH

Using quantitative and qualitative research methods, such as surveys, interviews, and available statistical information, I hope to discover, drive, and choose new directions and opportunities for the products I work with.

USER EMPATHY

Empathy and compassion are key throughout the whole design process. I put this into practice by creating user personas, empathy maps, and practicing sincere observation and listening skills during user interviews. This a skill I most recently had to apply as I learned to do video call interviews.

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

Planning an interview is just as important as conducting the interview itself. I think of the interview as a research study and a clear goal/s needs to be set for the results to be fruitful. Most importantly, I also strive to make the user as comfortable as possible by creating a natural rapport and guiding them through the process

MY WORK

LOKAL TRAVEL APP

Roles: UX researcher, UX designer, UI designer.

Current travelers are not able to safely travel by airplane, yet they are still looking to take advantage of the abundant amount of time they have during their new work from home life. Travelers know that traveling nearby and planning outdoor activities is the safest way to travel and maintain social distancing regulations. Lokal is an outdoor travel app that helps the modern travel enthusiast book safe trips locally.

PLANT THERAPY APP

Roles: UX researcher, UX designer, UI designer.

Plant Therapy was an app created around the growing plant enthusiast community. The Plant Therapy app connects plant owners and experts to easily and efficiently improve plant care.

CHILD WELFARE WEBSITE

Roles: UX researcher, UX designer, UI designer.

With responsive redesign in mind, we reimagined the Child Welfare government website for mobile and desktop. Our goal was to reorganize and simplify the navigation to help users quickly find and use the crucial resources provided by the website.
